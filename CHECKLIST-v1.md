## Success Brief

The site succeeds if:

1. A hiring manager lands on `/` and within 30 seconds understands: who you are, what problems you solve, and why you're not a junior.
2. At least one case study shows a real decision you made — not just polished screens.
3. The visual language feels like it came from a product designer, not a motion designer or a developer.
4. There is nothing to remove. No fluff sections, no vague copy.

The site fails if:
- The hero is generic ("I design digital products")
- Case studies are portfolio dumps — screens without thinking
- It looks like a Dribbble shot: beautiful but contentless
- Mobile is broken
